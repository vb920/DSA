# 905. Sort Array By Parity
**Pattern:** Two Pointers | **Difficulty:** Easy | **Time:** 5m | **Solo:** Y (Brute) / N (Optimal)

* **The Trick:** First even then odd. To do it without extra space, swap them in-place.
* **Execution:**
  1. Left pointer at start, right pointer at reverse (end).
  2. If left is odd and right is even -> Swap them.
  3. Move left forward if it's even, move right backward if it's odd.
* **Complexity:** Time `O(N)` | Space `O(1)`
